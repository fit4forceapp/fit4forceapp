General Questions

What is FIT4FORCE?

FIT4FORCE is Nigeria's premier mobile app designed to help aspiring candidates prepare for recruitment into Nigerian military and paramilitary organizations. It provides comprehensive study materials, practice quizzes, mock exams, fitness training, and AI-powered coaching to maximize your chances of success.

Which agencies does FIT4FORCE support?

FIT4FORCE supports preparation for 11 Nigerian agencies:

  • Armed Forces: Nigerian Army, Nigerian Navy, Nigerian Air Force
  • Military Education: Nigerian Defence Academy (NDA), Direct Short Service Commission (DSSC/SSC)
  • Law Enforcement: Police Academy (POLAC)
  • Paramilitary Services: Federal Fire Service, Nigeria Security and Civil Defence Corps (NSCDC), Nigeria Customs Service, Nigeria Immigration Service, Federal Road Safety Corps (FRSC)

Is FIT4FORCE available on my device?

FIT4FORCE is available on Android, iOS, and Web. You can download it from the Google Play Store, Apple App Store, or access it directly through your web browser.

Is FIT4FORCE free to use?

Yes! FIT4FORCE offers a free tier with access to essential study materials and basic features. Premium features, including unlimited mock exams, AI coaching, and advanced analytics, are available through our affordable subscription plans.
